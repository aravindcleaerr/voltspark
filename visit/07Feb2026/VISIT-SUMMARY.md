## Visit Outcome

### Meeting could NOT be conducted as per the planned agenda.

**Primary Reason:** There are **NO automatic energy measurement devices/tools** in place at Unnathi CNC. All energy monitoring and auditing is done **entirely manually** (paper-based registers, manual EB bill tracking, handwritten records).

This means:
- Our software system's value proposition of **automating** energy data capture does not directly apply in its current form
- The client needs a solution that works with **manual data entry** workflows first
- There is an opportunity to recommend IoT/smart metering as a future upgrade

## Implications for Our Energy Management System

Given that Unnathi has **no automatic metering**, our system needs to support:

1. **Manual data entry workflows** — operators enter daily meter readings by hand
2. **EB bill data import** — monthly data from electricity bills entered manually
3. **Solar production logging** — manual entry from solar inverter readings
4. **Simple forms** — quick entry screens for daily/weekly/monthly consumption
5. **Calculated analytics** — derive insights from manually entered data (trends, vs-target, deviations)
6. **Print-ready reports** — formatted for ZED assessor review

**Future upgrade path:**
- Recommend IoT energy meters at key load points
- Integrate with solar inverter data APIs
- Automated data capture to eliminate manual entry
