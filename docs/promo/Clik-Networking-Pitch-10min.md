## SLIDE 1: Opening Hook (1 min)

## SLIDE 2: The Problem (1.5 min)

> "Electronics manufacturers face 3 problems that nobody is solving:"

**1. Energy costs are invisible**
- SMT lines, reflow ovens, wave soldering, ATE — all heavy consumers
- No visibility into which process costs how much
- Hidden penalties — PF, demand overshoot, tariff mismatches in BESCOM bills
- ₹8-9/unit grid cost is eating into already thin margins

**2. Compliance is becoming mandatory**
- ZED certification — MSME ministry pushing hard, linked to subsidies & GeM procurement
- ISO 50001 (Energy Management) — customers like Bosch, Continental, L&T asking for it
- Electrical Safety audits — insurance companies demanding it
- Currently tracked in Excel files, WhatsApp photos, email threads

**3. Can't prove improvements**
- You install an APFC panel or switch to LED — but can you prove the ₹ savings?
- Your energy consultant gives you advice — but where's the ROI data?
- OEM customers ask "what's your energy efficiency?" — no data to show

---

## SLIDE 3: The Solution — VoltSpark (2 min)

> "We built **VoltSpark** — a cloud platform that gives electronics manufacturers complete visibility into energy, compliance, and savings."

**What it does:**
- **Utility Bill Analyzer** — enter your BESCOM bill → instantly see PF penalties, demand overshoot, anomalies flagged in red
- **Energy Tracking** — monitor grid, solar, DG consumption monthly → spot trends, compare year-on-year
- **Compliance Dashboard** — ZED, ISO 50001, Electrical Safety checklists → tick off requirements, attach evidence, see score
- **Savings Tracker** — installed VFD on your compressor? Track actual ₹ saved month-by-month. Prove ROI.
- **ROI Calculator** — considering solar rooftop or APFC panel? Get payback period, NPV, IRR instantly
- **Safety Modules** — inspection checklists, incident reports, certification expiry alerts
- **Multi-tenant** — if you're a consultant, manage 10+ electronics clients from one dashboard

> "Think of it as **Tally for energy & compliance** — simple, no hardware needed, built for Indian industry."

**"We already have an energy consultant."**
> "Perfect — VoltSpark makes your consultant more effective. They can track all recommendations, prove savings, generate reports. Ask them to try it — they'll love the portfolio dashboard."
