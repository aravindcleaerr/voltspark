The Problem 1:30

"Electronics manufacturers face 3 problems that nobody is solving:"

1. Energy Costs Are Invisible

  • SMT lines, reflow ovens, wave soldering, ATE — all heavy consumers
  • No visibility into which process costs how much
  • Hidden penalties — PF, demand overshoot, tariff mismatches in BESCOM bills
  • ₹8–9/unit grid cost is eating into already thin margins

2. Compliance is Becoming Mandatory

  • ZED certification — MSME ministry pushing hard, linked to subsidies & GeM procurement

  • Currently tracked in Excel files, WhatsApp photos, email threads

3. Can't Prove Improvements

  • Install APFC panel or switch to LED — but can you prove the ₹ savings?
  • Energy consultant gives advice — but where's the ROI data?
  • OEM customers ask "what's your energy efficiency?" — no data to show

Backup Q&A Answers

"How is this different from SAP / Schneider EcoStruxure?"
Those are ₹10–50 lakh enterprise tools for Tata/Reliance. VoltSpark is built for MSMEs — ₹3K/month, no hardware, no IT team needed. Start in 5 minutes.
"Does it need IoT sensors?"
No. Start with monthly bill data — that alone gives you 80% of insights. IoT integration is on the roadmap for companies that want real-time monitoring.
"What about data security?"
Multi-tenant with row-level isolation. Your data is completely separate. Hosted on enterprise cloud. No data mixing.
"We already have an energy consultant."
Perfect — VoltSpark makes your consultant more effective. They can track recommendations, prove savings, generate reports. Ask them to try it.
"Can it handle our specific compliance needs?"
Compliance frameworks are configurable. ZED, ISO 50001, and Electrical Safety are built-in. We can add industry-specific requirements.
"We're a small unit, is it worth it?"
The smaller you are, the more ₹8–9/unit electricity hurts margins. Even 5% reduction on ₹3L/month bill = ₹15K/month saved = ₹1.8L/year.
