## SLIDE 2: What is ZED Certification?

**ZED = Zero Defect Zero Effect**

- Government of India initiative for MSMEs
- Promotes quality manufacturing with zero defects
- Ensures minimal environmental impact (zero effect)
- **4 Levels:** Bronze → Silver → Gold → Diamond

**Energy Management** is a critical parameter across all ZED levels.

---

## SLIDE 3: ZED Energy Management — 5 Key Requirements

| # | Requirement | What Assessor Checks |
|---|------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Energy Source Identification & Targets** | Register of all energy sources, reduction targets set |
| 2 | **Consumption Monitoring** | Daily/periodic recording, deviation analysis |
| 3 | **Employee Training** | Energy awareness training with attendance records |
| 4 | **Periodic Energy Audits** | Internal audits with documented findings |
| 5 | **Root Cause Analysis & CAPA** | Systematic problem-solving with evidence of closure |

**All 5 must be documented with evidence for the assessor.**

## SLIDE 4: The Gap — Where Most Companies Struggle

### Common Issues During ZED Assessment:

❌ Energy sources identified but not formally documented
❌ Consumption tracked only via monthly EB bills — no daily monitoring
❌ No defined targets or deviation analysis
❌ Training conducted but no attendance records maintained
❌ No formal internal energy audits
❌ Problems fixed but no documented CAPA process
❌ Records scattered in different registers/files

### The Result:
- Assessor gives "Not Conforming" on energy parameters
- Certification delayed or level downgraded

## SLIDE 6: Module Overview

### 1. Energy Source Register
- Add all energy sources: Electricity (EB), DG set, Diesel, Compressed Air, LPG, etc.
- Record meter numbers, locations, units of measurement
- Link targets to each source

### 2. Consumption Monitoring & Deviation Detection
- Record daily consumption readings
- System automatically compares against targets
- **>10% deviation = Warning** (yellow alert)
- **>20% deviation = Critical** (red alert — CAPA recommended)
- Shift-wise recording support (Morning/Afternoon/Night)

### 3. Energy Targets
- Set monthly/quarterly/annual reduction targets
- Track actual vs target with visual progress
- Baseline comparison for year-on-year improvement

### 4. Training Management
- Schedule energy awareness training programs
- Mark attendance digitally
- Track completion rates across the organization
- Generate training compliance reports

### 5. Audit Management
- Schedule internal energy audits
- Record findings with categories: Observation, Minor NC, Major NC, Opportunity
- Track finding closure with due dates
- Link findings to CAPA for systematic resolution

### 6. CAPA (Corrective & Preventive Actions)
- Raise CAPA from audit findings or consumption deviations
- **5-Why Root Cause Analysis** built-in
- Track corrective and preventive actions
- Verification and closure workflow
- Full discussion thread for collaboration

### 7. Reports
- Energy Source Register Report
- Consumption Summary Report
- Training Compliance Report
- Audit Summary Report
- CAPA Summary Report
- **ZED Compliance Overview** — single-page status of all 5 requirements
