## TALKING POINTS & RESPONSES

### "Why do we need a software system for energy management?"
**Response:** ZED certification requires documented evidence of systematic energy management — not just one-time reports. The assessor will check for:
- Identified energy sources with registers
- Monthly/quarterly consumption tracking with targets
- Evidence of deviation analysis and corrective actions
- Training records with attendance
- Periodic audit reports with findings
- CAPA (Corrective and Preventive Actions) with closure evidence

A manual system is possible but difficult to maintain and present during assessment. Our system automates all of this and generates print-ready reports.

### "What is the cost?"
**Response:** Discuss with Lakshminarasimhan Sir on pricing. The system runs on their own server/computer — no recurring cloud costs. One-time setup + training + support during assessment.

### "How long will implementation take?"
**Response:** The system is already built. Implementation involves:
- Day 1-2: Data entry of energy sources, employees, historical data
- Week 1: Training for key personnel on daily usage
- Week 2-4: Daily usage and monitoring
- Month 2: First internal audit using the system
- Month 3: Assessment-ready

### "Can we access it from mobile?"
**Response:** Yes, it's a responsive web application. Works on any device with a browser — phone, tablet, laptop. No app installation needed.
